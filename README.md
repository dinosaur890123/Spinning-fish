# Spinning fish
An original browser based clicker game where you spin a fish as much as you can! 
First of its kind (very original)

Try asking the FishAI (pre-programmed) to 'stop' or 'logout' or even 'Why are you spinning'
Who knows what it will respond with...

## Gameplay
Just click the "Spin the fish" button and accumulate coins!
Spend your coins on upgrades/boosts in the shop, and watch the disaster meter rise. The higher the disaster meter, the more chaotic things fall

## Upgrades
Gold fish - 50 - Turns the fish gold and raises disaster
Speed boost - 150 - Increases spin speed, and raises disaster
Coin multiplier - 200 - Earns more coins per tick while spinning. The fish accepts this.
Auto spin - 300 - Fish spins without input. No going back :)
Scam - 500 - You get nothing!

## Features
- Disaster meter
- FishAI (pre-programmed responses)
- Stats panel (Displays RPM, G force, and a synergy value)
